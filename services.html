Services


FULL PRODUCT ASSEMBLY

Surface mount technology (SMT) is a manufacturing technique that uses machines to attach electronic components directly to a printed circuit board (PCB). The components, called surface-mount devices (SMDs), are soldered onto the board using a batch solder-reflow process.


PRINTED BOARD ASSEMBLY

Printed circuit boards (PCBs) are the backbone of modern electronics. From smartphones to computers to medical devices, PCBs provide the foundation on which components are mounted and connected. As electronic products become more advanced, PCB design and manufacturing has grown increasingly complex.


3D PRINTING

Engineers and designers build realistic computer models of parts and assemblies for complex simulations and digital manufacturing. Models created with CAD can be produced as physical components with 3D printing.
